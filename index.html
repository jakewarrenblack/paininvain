ABOUT PAIN IN VAIN

PAIN IN VAIN is a Metal band founded by Mikey O' Riordan and Jake Black during summer 2014. They have gigged throughout many locations in Ireland and have supported notable high profile acts such as I Set My Friends On Fire and Follow My Lead. Based in Ireland's capital, PAIN IN VAIN take influence from bands such as Bring Me The Horizon, Darkest Hour, Suicide Silence, The Black Dahlia Murder, Amity Affliction and Of Mice and Men. Debut album, 'Immolation' out now!
